# Monitor Dashboard Camera Status

**Date:** 2026-04-15

## Working
- **PTZ controls** — responsive, low latency (~0.3ms) after daemon optimization
- **Zoom controls** — working correctly
- **Snapshot capture** — reliable after switching to `v4l2-ctl` primary with ffmpeg fallback, plus automatic usb_camera stop/restart around capture

- **System metrics** (CPU/RAM/Temp) — displaying in header

This was left unresolved to move on.
